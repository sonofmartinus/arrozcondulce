Arroz Con Dulce

Richard Martinez, Jan 1, 0001

Arroz Con Dulce Arroz con dulce, a beloved Puerto Rican dessert, is a creamy and comforting rice pudding. It’s crafted with tender rice cooked in a fragrant blend of cinnamon, ginger, and other warm spices. Enriched with coconut milk, evaporated milk, and sugar, it boasts a rich and indulgent flavor. Raisins are often added for a touch of sweetness and texture. This versatile dessert can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, and its thick, custardy consistency makes it a delightful treat to savor with a spoon or even with the hands.
